# Wikidata: State of the Art

Explore a sample of over 9,000 paintings extracted from Wikidata. Learn more about these paintings in [this data story](https://uclab.fh-potsdam.de/inflect/artonwikidata/). The data was collected in December 2025. The paintings had to include an image, a creation date, and minimal metadata. Additionally, they had to be referenced on at least three Wikimedia sites.

![tags](img/infobar_tags.svg)

The facets above give access to the most prominent artists, countries, depicted entities, genres, materials, movements, and museums of the paintings. Click on the keywords to narrow down the selection of artworks.

![time](img/infobar_time.svg)

The artworks are arranged by decade of creation. 

![time](img/infobar_scroll.svg)

You can zoom in on the artworks using your touchpad or scroll wheel to see more details. Click and drag the background to adjust the visible area.
